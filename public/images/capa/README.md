# Capa catalog logos

Brand marks shown on the Up homepage for the capabilities checked into Capa.

- Box, Discord, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Kubernetes, Sentry, Stripe, Twitch, and Zoom: downloaded from the Simple Icons CDN on 2026-06-23.
- Slack and Twilio: pinned from `simple-icons@13.21.0` because those marks are no longer available from the live Simple Icons CDN.

These marks identify their respective services. All trademarks belong to their owners. The catalog source of truth is `acoyfellow/capa/capabilities/*/capa.manifest.json`; Twilio represents three checked-in bindings: core, Messaging, and Verify.
